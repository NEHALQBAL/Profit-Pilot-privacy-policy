Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 4, 2026

ProfitPilot is a financial analysis application that helps users process payout, inventory, and sales-related files to calculate profit, loss, and business insights. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, how we protect it, and what choices you have.

1. Introduction

We value your privacy and are committed to handling your business data responsibly. ProfitPilot is designed to work primarily with local device storage and to minimize the collection of personal information.

This Privacy Policy applies to:

  • the ProfitPilot mobile or desktop application,
  • any related backend services used for temporary processing,
  • any website or documentation that links to this policy.

2. Information We Collect

We collect only the information necessary to provide the core features of the app.

  • Files you upload or import: such as payout, inventory, sales, CSV, Excel, or other business files used for analysis.
  • App usage data: basic technical information such as crashes, performance data, and feature usage, if enabled through app diagnostics or similar tools.
  • Device information: general device and operating system details needed to keep the app working properly.
  • Optional support information: details you send to us when contacting support, such as your email address and any message you choose to share.

We do not collect more data than is needed for app functionality and support.

3. How We Use Information

We use information only for legitimate app-related purposes, including:

  • processing business files and generating profit/loss calculations,
  • showing charts, reports, and insights inside the app,
  • saving user preferences and app settings,
  • fixing bugs, improving performance, and maintaining app stability,
  • responding to support requests,
  • meeting legal or security requirements when necessary.

4. Local Storage and File Processing

ProfitPilot is built to work mainly on your device. In many cases, files are processed locally and are not permanently stored on our servers.

If temporary server-side processing is used for a feature, the data is handled only for the time needed to complete that task and is not used for unrelated purposes.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information or business files.

We may share limited information only in these situations:

  • with service providers that help us operate the app,
  • to comply with applicable laws, regulations, or lawful requests,
  • to protect the rights, safety, or security of users and the app,
  • during a business transfer such as a merger or acquisition, if applicable.

6. Third-Party Services

If ProfitPilot uses third-party services such as analytics, crash reporting, cloud storage, or payment processing, those services may process limited data according to their own privacy policies.

You should review the privacy policies of any third-party services used by the app.

7. Permissions

ProfitPilot may request access only to permissions needed for its features. For example, file access may be required to import and analyze your business documents.

Any permission you grant is used only for the feature you selected.

8. Data Retention

We keep information only as long as needed to provide the app, support its features, or meet legal obligations.

If your files are stored locally on your device, you can remove them by deleting the file or clearing app data, depending on how the app works.

9. Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect information against unauthorized access, misuse, loss, or disclosure. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure, so we cannot guarantee absolute security.

10. Your Choices and Rights

Depending on your location and the way you use the app, you may have the right to:

  • access the information you provided,
  • request correction or deletion where applicable,
  • withdraw permission for certain device access,
  • stop using the app and remove locally stored files from your device.

11. Children’s Privacy

ProfitPilot is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us information, please contact us and we will take appropriate steps.

12. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The updated version will be posted on this page with a new “Last Updated” date.
